Fastest Cars in the World

Below are some of the fastest cars ever produced in the world.

1. Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut

The Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut is the pinnacle of Swedish engineering. Created by Christian von Koenigsegg, the Jesko Absolut is the fastest car in the world, with a top speed of 330 mph.

2. Bugatti Chiron Pur Sport

The Bugatti Chiron Pur Sport is the second fastest car in the world. It is made by the French car manufacturer Bugatti. They previously held the record for the fastest car in the world with the Bugatti Veyron.

3. Hennessey Venom F5

The folks in the USA are smashing the top speed game recently, with fourth place going to another all-American supercar. The Hennessey Venom F5 has a 6.6-litre twin-turbocharged V8 with 1,817hp.
